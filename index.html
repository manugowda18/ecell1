"OUR MISSION"

Our motto is to convert feasible ideas into actual business propositions which will not only add value to individuals but will also benefit the nation as a whole. The goal is to enable India, which plays a key role in the global IT sector. This acts as a catalyst in transforming ideas to business start-up which in turn strengthens the innovation-based entrepreneurship development infrastructure. To create an environment that would ignite the ideas on innovation and creative thinking to cultivate an entrepreneurship culture and to reach out to a large audience.

Entrepreneurship literally creates fresh money. Existing enterprises may be limited to the breadth of existing markets and may reach a financial ceiling. Entrepreneurs' new and improved offerings, goods, or technology enable the development of new markets and the creation of new riches. Furthermore, the cascading impact of increasing employment and higher incomes contributes to higher national income through increased tax collection and government spending. The government can utilise this revenue to invest in other, struggling sectors and human capital. Although it may cause a few existing players to lose their jobs, the government can mitigate the impact by diverting surplus wealth to retrain people.
